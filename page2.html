Digital Addiction and Adults


Kids have digital addiction. But, adults are as guilty as kids and teens. Recent studies have shown that adults use their cell phones and screens as much as kids and teens. Adults said that they read less books because of smartphones.



Most adults don't play games or are on social media every second of the the day. Adults have digital addiction when it comes to texts and emails. Most adults keep their phone next to them just so they can check their email.






Tips for adults:



1. Do not keep your phone next to you when you sleep.

2. Have a policy where you don't touch your phone until a certain time.

3. Keep your phone away from you when you are having fun with your family.

Digital Addiction is becoming a problem! Please try to prevent it!
