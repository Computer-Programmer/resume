Tips/Tricks on preventing digital addiction
Digital Addiction is becoming a big problem in today's world and here's how to prevent it:



1. Put electronic devices away. Leave your phone in the other room when you are doing your homework or cooking, or whatever you are busy with.

2. Have no electronics until a certain time. Make a hard and fast rule that you will not pick up your phone or your computer until a certain time.

3. Remove applications. Log out of, or uninstall, most applications you may have active on your phone.

4. When you need to walk somewhere, experiment with leaving your headphones in your pocket- Decide not to listen to music or podcasts. Don't look at your phone. Enjoy the simplicity of walking without distractions.

5. Put your phone on airplane mode the night before. That way, if you need to briefly use your phone (to check the time or the weather or something), you won't get hit with a billion notifications.

6. Refrain from putting TV and electronic gadgets in your child's bedroom, and put away such devices after use.

7. Observe tech-free times such as during meals, homework and bedtime. In addition, you can designate tech-free zones for your child such as in the bedroom, dining area and in the car.

8. Challenge yourself to read at least 30 pages of a great book before you check your computer or mobile device. Pick the right reading material and you will soon find you have created a new pastime!

9. Skip the morning digital check-in.- Waking up to a techie breakfast of checking your email, Facebook or Instagram likes that you scored since midnight isn't that nourishing. It saps productivity by flooding the mind with a full plate of everything before you've had a chance to focus on the bigger picture.
